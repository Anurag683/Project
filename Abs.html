Targeted Core Workouts

Plank

  • Forearms on ground, body in straight line
  • Tighten core and glutes
  • 3 sets of 45–60 seconds
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Bicycle Crunches

  • Lying on your back, lift knees and shoulders
  • Alternate elbow to opposite knee
  • 3 sets of 20 reps per side
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Russian Twists

  • Sit down, lift feet slightly
  • Twist torso side to side with/without weight
  • 3 sets of 40 reps (20 each side)
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Leg Raises

  • Lie flat, hands under glutes
  • Lift legs without arching back
  • 3 sets of 15 reps
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Mountain Climbers

  • High plank position, drive knees forward
  • Stay fast, controlled, and light on your toes
  • 3 sets of 30 seconds
🎥 Watch Tutorial

V-Ups

  • Lie on back with arms and legs extended
  • Lift torso and legs together to form a “V”
  • 3 sets of 12 reps
