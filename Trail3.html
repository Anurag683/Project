Gym Terminology

BMR: Basal Metabolic Rate - The amount of energy expended by the body at rest to maintain basic physiological functions.
BMI: Body Mass Index - A measure of body fat based on height and weight.
Bulking: A phase of training and nutrition aimed at increasing muscle mass and size.
Cutting: A phase of training and nutrition aimed at reducing body fat while preserving muscle mass.
Calisthenics: Bodyweight exercises performed without the use of additional equipment, focusing on strength, flexibility, and body control.
Cardio: Aerobic exercises designed to improve cardiovascular health and endurance.
Conditioning: Improving cardiovascular fitness, endurance, and overall physical conditioning.
Core: The muscles of the abdomen, lower back, and pelvis that stabilize and support the spine.
Drop Set: A technique where weight is reduced between sets to extend the duration of a working set and increase muscle fatigue.
Failure: Inability to complete a repetition or set with proper form due to muscle fatigue.
