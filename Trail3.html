Gym Terminology

BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate - Energy your body uses at rest to maintain life functions.

BMI

Body Mass Index - A calculation of body fat based on height and weight.

Bulking

Training and nutrition phase to increase muscle mass and size.

Cutting

Phase focused on fat loss while maintaining muscle mass.

Calisthenics

Bodyweight training to build strength, flexibility, and control.

Cardio

Aerobic exercise that improves heart and lung health.

Conditioning

Enhancing overall fitness, endurance, and recovery ability.

Core

Muscles that stabilize the spine - abs, back, and pelvis.

Drop Set

Training method where you reduce weight during a set to increase fatigue.

Failure

The point at which a muscle can no longer complete a rep with proper form.
