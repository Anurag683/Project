Balanced Body Diet Plan

Fuel your body with balanced meals to support muscle growth and recovery.

Breakfast

  • Oatmeal with chia seeds and berries
  • Boiled eggs or Greek yogurt
  • Green tea or a protein shake

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast or tofu
  • Brown rice or quinoa
  • Steamed broccoli and carrots

Snacks

  • Almonds or walnuts
  • Apple slices with peanut butter
  • Low-sugar protein bar

Dinner

  • Grilled salmon or paneer
  • Mixed green salad with olive oil
  • Sweet potato or whole wheat chapati

Step-by-Step Meal Prep

Breakfast

  1. Boil eggs or prep Greek yogurt.
  2. Cook oatmeal and add chia seeds & berries.
  3. Brew green tea or shake your protein drink.
