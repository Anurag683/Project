Core Crusher Workouts

Strengthen your core muscles for better stability and posture.

Plank

Static core hold to build endurance and stability.

Russian Twists

Twisting side-to-side with a weight to engage obliques.

Bicycle Crunches

Targets upper and lower abs with alternating elbow-knee movements.

Core Crunches Workouts

Standard Crunches

  • Lie flat, knees bent, hands behind head
  • Crunch up using only your abs
  • 3 sets of 20–30 reps
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Bicycle Crunches

  • Alternate elbow to opposite knee
  • Twist through the core, not neck
  • 3 sets of 20 reps each side
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Russian Twists

  • Sit with feet lifted and twist torso side to side
  • Use a weight or just bodyweight
  • 3 sets of 40 twists (20 per side)
🎥 Watch Tutorial

Leg Raises

  • Lie flat, hands under glutes
  • Raise legs slowly, don’t arch back
  • 3 sets of 15 reps

Plank

  • Hold body in straight line on elbows
  • Engage glutes and core
  • 3 rounds of 1-minute holds

V-Ups

  • Start flat, raise hands and feet to meet
  • Explode up, control the descent
  • 3 sets of 12–15 reps
