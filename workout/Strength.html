Strength Training Workouts

Build muscle and increase strength with these effective exercises.

Bench Press

Targets: Chest, Triceps, Shoulders

  • 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Use a barbell or dumbbells
  • Maintain controlled movement

Deadlifts

Targets: Back, Glutes, Hamstrings

  • 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Keep your spine neutral
  • Engage your core throughout

Squats

Targets: Quads, Glutes, Core

  • 4 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Use bodyweight, dumbbells, or barbell
  • Keep knees aligned with toes

Shoulder Press

Targets: Shoulders, Triceps

  • 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Use dumbbells or barbell
  • Keep wrists aligned above elbows
